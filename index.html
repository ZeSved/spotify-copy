Hangman instructions:

  • The system will give you a random word from the english dictionary of any length.
  • Inside the box with "Type your guess here" you type either a single letter or an entire word if you think you know what it is, if you are correct, the _ will be replaced with the letter or the word.
  • If you press the "Generate a new word" button, a new word will be generated and all guesses will be reset.
  • Those 5 boxes are your lifes, when you get a letter/word wrong one box becomes white (checked). if all 5 boxes are checked you lose.
  • Good luck!
